And both the arbitrators thus appointed shall be appoint the third arbitrator who shall function as the presiding arbitrator. The arbitration shall be conducted at Noida in the English language. Any arbitration award shall include attorney’s fees for the prevailing party. The courts in the city of Noida shall have exclusive jurisdiction to entertain try and determined any dispute.
